\textbf{Log-Trick (Identity):}
\( \nabla_\theta \left[p_\theta(\vx)\right] =  p_\theta(\vx)\nabla_\theta \left[\log(p_\theta(\vx))\right] \)

\Thm[Cauchy-Schwarz]

\(\forall\vu,\vv\in V\colon\scprod{\vu,\vv}\leq\abs{\scprod{\vu,\vv}}\leq\norm{\vu}\norm{\vv}\).

\(\forall\vu,\vv\in V\colon 0\leq\abs{\scprod{\vu,\vv}}\leq\norm{\vu}\norm{\vv}\).

Special case: \((\sum x_i y_i)^2\leq(\sum x_i^2)(\sum y_i^2)\).

Special case: \(\Exp{XY}^2\leq\Exp{X^2}\Exp{Y^2}\).

\sep

\Thm[Fundamental Theorem of Calculs]

\(f(\vy) -  f(\vx) =  \int_{\gamma[\vx,\vy]} \nabla f(\vtau)\cdot d\vtau =  \int_{t = 0}^1\nabla f(\vgamma(t))^\T\vgamma'(t)\,dt\)

\(f(\vy) -  f(\vx) =  \int_{0}^1 \nabla f((1 - t)\vx + t\vy)^\T(\vy - \vx)\,dt\)

\Com Create a path \(\vgamma\) from \(\vx\) to \(\vy\) and integrate the dot product of the gradient of the function-values at the path with the derivative of the path.
